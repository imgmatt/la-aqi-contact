How this works

  1. Your address is geocoded by the U.S. Census / Esri public geocoder to a map point.
  2. That point is matched against the City of Los Angeles official Council District boundaries (LA GeoHub).
  3. We pair your district with its councilmember's public contact info and a pre-written letter you can edit and send.

Sources & data

Boundaries & councilmembers: LA GeoHub. Contacts: lacity.gov. Fire & air-quality reporting via ABC7, LAist, KTLA, NBC LA.
